# More Books Design Demos

Approval-only demos for the six proposed book SVG designs.

Open:

- `Plans/MoreBooksDesignDemos.html`

Notes:

- These demos are not wired into the app.
- They use the same intended `210 x 160` SVG coordinate system.
- They keep a visible right-side page block on every design.
- They keep the title in a protected middle panel.
- They do not show author text.
- They do not show a footer strip.
- Hover a demo book to preview the slight open effect.

Designs:

1. Cloth Hardback
2. Modern Paperback
3. Technical Manual
4. Decorative Hardcover
5. Library Ledger
6. Field Notebook
